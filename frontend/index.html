FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What datasets were used to train ForensicAI? +
Fake News module uses real news articles from Kaggle containing verified fake and real news. Deepfake module uses 140K Real and Fake Faces dataset from Kaggle, containing StyleGAN-generated faces.
How accurate is the deepfake detection? +
Our EfficientNet-B0 model achieved 83.4% validation accuracy after 5 epochs of training on CPU. With GPU training and more epochs, this can reach 90%+.
What is Grad-CAM and why does it matter? +
Grad-CAM (Gradient-weighted Class Activation Mapping) creates a heatmap showing which regions of an image influenced the model's decision. This makes AI decisions transparent and explainable.
Can ForensicAI detect videos? +
Currently ForensicAI detects deepfake images. Video detection is planned for the next version — it will extract frames and analyze each one using the same model.
Is my data safe? +
Yes. ForensicAI runs locally on your machine. No data is sent to external servers — everything is processed by the local Flask API running on your computer.
